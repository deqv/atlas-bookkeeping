About

Hi, I’m Varun — and I help home service business owners pay less in taxes, stay audit-proof, and finally understand their numbers.

Before founding Atlas Bookkeeping, I worked as an acquisition specialist helping small businesses sell. The #1 issue? Messy books that lowered business valuations and scared off buyers.

That’s why I transitioned into accounting. Today, I specialize in monthly bookkeeping and tax optimization for service businesses like plumbing, HVAC, and electrical. I hold a Master’s in Accounting, QuickBooks ProAdvisor certification, and I’m on track to complete my CPA.

I only take on a few clients at a time so I can give each one focused attention and real results. If you're earning good money but unsure where it all goes—or worried you're overpaying the IRS—we should talk.
